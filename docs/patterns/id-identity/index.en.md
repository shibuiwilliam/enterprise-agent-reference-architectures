# Plane 3: Identity & Trust (ID) ★ Most Challenging

Faithful propagation of permissions. Guaranteeing which identity's authority an agent acts under. Combining OBO delegation, permission mirroring, JIT credentials, and PDP/PEP in environments with tens of thousands of users and many SaaS systems makes this the most architecturally demanding of the seven planes.
